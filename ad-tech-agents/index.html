Architecture

Building six agents is a weekend project. Making them work together at a bank is what you pay us for.

Most "AI agent" pitches stop at the agent. The hard part is what sits underneath — the definitions, the data contracts, the governance. Without a shared semantic layer, agents make decisions on inconsistent inputs and contradict each other in production. We build the foundation before we build the agents. Every engagement.

03 Coaching insight · field gap detected post-call analysis · pattern lookup
MEDDIC gap · 1 of 7 fields unfilled

Champion not surfaced in 30 minutes of discovery.

Marcus Lee was named as Technical Buyer. David Chen was confirmed as Economic Buyer. But no Champion emerged — no one explicitly committed to advocating for this deal day-to-day inside Northwind. Historical pattern across 247 closed-won and closed-lost deals: opportunities without an identified Champion by end of discovery slip 3.2× more often by proposal stage. This is the highest-leverage thing to fix before the technical deep-dive.

Recommended actions · 3
1
Add to technical deep-dive agenda
Direct question to ask Marcus, since he'll be in the room: "What does success look like for the team in 6 months — what would have to be true for this to be the obvious choice?"
2
Subtle probe in the follow-up email
Insert toward end of recap email — soft, not transactional: "Anyone else on your team you'd want included in the next conversation?"

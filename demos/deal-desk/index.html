03 Critical clause · § 14.1 deep-dive precedent matched · counter-language drafted

Limitation of Liability · 3× cap with uncapped regulated-data exception

DataForge standard (§ 14.1)
Customer's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Customer during the twelve (12) months preceding the claim, except for breach of confidentiality, gross negligence, IP indemnification, and willful misconduct.
Northwind redline
DataForge's total aggregate liability shall not exceed three times (3×) the annual fees, with uncapped liability for any security incident involving regulated data (defined as PHI, PII, or trial-related research data).
